Did you know...

"A serving of cucumber -- one-half a cup -- is about 8 calories"

(see "Cucumber" from WebMD)

  • Half of a medium or large cucumber, or a small whole one
  • Feta cheese
  • Your favorite vinaigrette dressing
This idea is a spin-off of/was inspired by this Greek Salad Stuffed Cucumber Bites recipe from the website Divalicious Recipes--feel free to check it out if you have a little more time to cook!

For this simplified version, wash and chop up your cucumber in slices or quarters, however you prefer (if you want tips on how to quarter a cucumber, visit the Tips & Tricks page). Then drizzle with the vinaigrette dressing, sprinkle with some feta cheese and enjoy your cucumber salad!
