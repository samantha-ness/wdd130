Overview

Purpose

Veggie University will be a quick online stop providing fast, easy ways to prep veggies in ways that actually taste good, for college students!

Audience

The website will be specifically designed for college students who are low on time, want to eat healthier, but don't want to eat veggies that taste gross or boring. The tips and ideas won't have long cook times or lengthy lists of expensive ingredients which students don't have time or money for. Instead, it will offer creative ways to use vegetables and help students be healthier in a practical and delicious way!

Home page

The Problem

When was the last time you ate a vegetable--and actually enjoyed it?

In the life of a college student, there's always one more thing we know we should be doing--but have a hard time getting to. For many, one of those is eating healthy, specifically enough vegetables! We've all been there before. Example: maybe you've found a vegetable recipe that looks delicious, and the blog says it'll only take 15 minutes. You eagerly get started only to realize that it's 15 minutes after an hour of washing, peeling and chopping (the ingredient conveniently called for everything to be pre-chopped). Or maybe you find some recipes that look tastier than just sprinkling your veggies with salt, but don't look too time-intensive. You give one a try, it turns out well, success! You promise yourself you'll do this at least once a week--and then you don't have time to actually cook again for the next 5 weeks. Or maybe, after enough of these experiences, you've given up and resigned to occasionally shoving a few raw carrot or celery sticks down your throat to assuage your guilt--or maybe you just don't do that ever, because let's be honest, who can think a more boring thing to eat!?

The Solution?

Often, we don't really know how to prepare our vegetables in ways that taste good--like, something we'd actually want to eat again. Or, maybe you do know some ways to make fantastic vegetable dishes--but how often do you actually have time to cook? Exactly. If you're here on this website, it's because you wish you could eat healthy, somehow make your veggies taste delicious, AND do it in a short amount of time. The good news? You've finally found the solution! Welcome to Veggie University!

So, why is Veggie University different?

Allow me to explain! First off, this website is designed by someone like you: a college student who knows how busy life can get, and wants a solution to eating healthier that actually is practical! I'm not a professional chef--these recipes aren't fast because I have wicked-fast cooking skills. Like you, I am also on a budget and can't afford to buy five thousand things for one recipe, no matter how delicious it would make my food taste. Like many of you, I really want to be healthy and feel great. But like everyone, I still just want to eat food that tastes good! So this website is going to help out with these needs by being real:

My veggie sides recipes are between 3 to 10 minutes long, and the veggie meals are between 10 and 30--ACTUALLY

For many of the recipes, I time myself making them for the first time, to make your time estimate as realistic as possible

I strive to use foods that the majority of college students are likely to have on hand, OR can easily find and are actually likely to reuse in other meals. My recipes go for the max flavor possible with as few ingredients as possible to save on time and money
